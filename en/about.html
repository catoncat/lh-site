01 / PROFILE

Company

Taishan City Lihua Electric Factory Ltd., founded in 1993. 30 years of EMS with supply-chain management, full manufacturing processes and engineering support. Execution standards shaped by work with global customers including Boeing, HP, IBM, Thermo, ABB and Emerson.

02 / CAMPUS

Campus

50-acre campus with 30 acres reserve; about 7,000 square meters of manufacturing space focused on stable delivery and consistent quality.

03 / QUALITY

Quality & Certifications

ISO 9001, ISO 14000, IATF 16949; workmanship to IPC-A-610 Class 2/3, IPC J-STD-001 and IPC/WHMA-A-620. IPC member company.

04 / LEAN

Lean Manufacturing

Lean is applied on appropriate product/process paths to improve efficiency and quality — not as decorative copy.
